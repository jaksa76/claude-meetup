# Bar Municipality – Citizen Issue Tracker

The application lets residents of Bar, Montenegro report problems in public spaces — potholes, broken street lighting, illegal dumping, damaged infrastructure — by pinning them on an interactive map and adding a description or photo.

Municipal staff review incoming reports through an admin dashboard, assign them to the relevant department, and update their status as work progresses. Citizens receive an SMS notification when their report is resolved.

The interface is available in Montenegrin and English, making it accessible to both locals and the international community living in Bar.
